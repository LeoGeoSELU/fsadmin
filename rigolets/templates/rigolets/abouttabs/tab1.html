Fishing Spots is a service being developed in collaboration with a local physicist. The aim of Fishing Spots is to combine real-time weather and water conditions with other environmental factors to calculate optimal fishing conditions for a particular location.

While the project will be a long-term, research-oriented endeavor, the scope of the project, for the sake of CMPS 411, will be limited to the Rigolets area within the Lake Pontchartrain Basin.
